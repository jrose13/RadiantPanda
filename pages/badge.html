Badge

Badge is a contacts and communication mobile app for companies. It allows teammates to communicate more effectively and see important contact and contextual information about their colleagues.

Badge creates a better way to share critical employee information, not only for connecting with each other, but also for surfacing contextual information, such as where a teammate fits within the organization, or if someone is available to chat. Badge was designed to become the central identity for employees, a mobile hub that connected all their access, conversations, interactions, and information related to work.
