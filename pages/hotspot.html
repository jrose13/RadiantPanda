Hotspot

Hanging out with friends is great but wrangling them together can be a pain in the ass. Jasjit Singh and I created Hotspot to make this process less painful.

Hotspot then evolved into a deals app where users are rewarded money off drinks and food for inviting their friends to join them at a bar or restaurant.

Coded the iOS app and the backend in Django. I stopped working on Hotspot in August 2014 but Jasjit carries the torch.
